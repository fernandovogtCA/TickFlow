About MarketBoard

Real-time markets.
Always on.

MarketBoard is a fullscreen financial display designed for offices, trading desks, co-working spaces, and any professional environment that needs live market data at a glance — no clutter, no noise, just numbers.

Whether it's a 65" screen in a trading room, a monitor in a financial advisory office, or a display in a crypto exchange lobby, MarketBoard turns any screen into a live market dashboard. Fully customizable — choose which assets to show, adjust font sizes, pick a color theme, and cast it to any Chromecast-enabled display.

MarketBoard tracks cryptocurrencies, US equity indices, commodities, and foreign exchange pairs — including major, Asian, and emerging market currencies — all updated in real time.
